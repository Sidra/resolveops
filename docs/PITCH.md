# ResolveOps — Pitch Document

**One-liner:**
"We replace your entire customer support department — tools, AI add-ons, and labor — with a single AI-native operation, governed by a policy engine so it can safely execute refunds, reships, and account changes."

---

## The Problem

Customer support today is three separate line items on every startup's balance sheet:

1. **Helpdesk / ticketing** — Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom ($50–$150/agent/mo)
2. **AI add-ons / bots** — Intercom Fin ($0.99/resolution), Zendesk AI, etc.
3. **Human labor** — in-house team or BPO agency ($8–$25/hr, Philippines/India)

Founders get "tool fatigue": they pay for software and still do the integration, prompting, staffing, training, QA, and escalations themselves. 77% of service agents report rising workload, 56% experience burnout, and 69% of CX leaders cite agent attrition as a major challenge. Meanwhile, 73% of consumers switch brands after repeated bad support experiences.

The result: startups spend 15–30% of revenue on customer support and still deliver inconsistent quality.

## The Solution

**ResolveOps** is an AI-native Customer Ops agency that takes operational ownership and delivers the outcome:

- **Takeover**: connect channels + knowledge + policies; go live in 1 day (not weeks/months)
- **Autonomy + HITL**: AI resolves 60%+ automatically; AI engineers handle edge cases; the ratio shifts over time
- **Execution, not chat**: the agent performs actions (refund/reship/cancel/address change) behind a policy engine with approvals and audit trails

Three balance sheet items become one. No ticketing system to manage. No AI tool to configure. No BPO team to hire/train/replace.

## Three Pillars

1. **Outcome ownership** — no operator burden on the customer
2. **Trust & control** — policy engine + approvals + audit replay
3. **Execution-first** — actions, not chats; refunds processed, not explained

Support becomes a revenue engine, not a cost line.
